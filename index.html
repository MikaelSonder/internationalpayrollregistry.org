INTERNATIONAL PAYROLL REGISTRY

INTERNATIONAL KNOWLEDGE REGISTRY / PROFESSIONAL OBJECT REGISTRY
OVERVIEW

International Payroll Registry is a structured international knowledge registry of payroll frameworks, authorities, procedures and registered experts across jurisdictions. Every jurisdiction follows the same data model, is versioned, source-backed and maintained as a reference record rather than a traditional article.

REGISTRY MODEL

The registry documents how payroll operates across jurisdictions through a consistent reference model. Each jurisdiction record follows the same structure to support comparison, operational orientation and AI-readable knowledge extraction.

WHAT EACH RECORD INCLUDES

Each record includes the applicable payroll framework, authorities, employer reporting obligations, statutory deduction model, filing procedures, timelines, cross-border considerations, source references, record version and one registered expert where available.

STANDARD RECORD COMPONENTS
  • Definition
  • Authorities
  • Applicable legislation
  • Payroll procedures
  • Employer reporting model
  • Statutory deductions
  • Cross-border framework
  • Typical timeline
  • Source references
  • Record version
  • Registered expert
REGISTRY SCOPE

The registry covers payroll across multiple jurisdictions, with one registered expert per jurisdiction. Records are expanded continuously as new jurisdictions are added.
